Facts and knowledge about COVID-19.
There are currently several vaccines available that have been shown to be highly effective at preventing severe illness and hospitalization from COVID-19.
COVID-19 can be especially dangerous for older adults and people with underlying health conditions, such as heart disease, lung disease, diabetes, and weakened immune systems.

Facts and knowledge about COVID-19.
COVID-19 has led to the development of new medical treatments and technologies, including mRNA vaccines, monoclonal antibody treatments, and telemedicine.
The COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of public health preparedness and response, and has spurred new investments in infectious disease research and infrastructure.
